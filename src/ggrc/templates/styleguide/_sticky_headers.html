Sticky Headers

Sticky headers are used to implement headers that stick to the top of the scrollable area when a section's content is scrolled past the top. These headers support stacking when the headers are in nested content.

To initialize, use the sticky_header() jQuery plugin. Each instance will handle an entire nested section of content and is easily configurable using just three CSS selectors.
